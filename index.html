Advantages

  • Safety and privacy

    Being open, Meduza OS takes precedence over proprietary software, exceeding its security and privacy. When source code is available for auditing, anyone, be it a security researcher, an interested user, or a vendor that supplies an OS on their hardware, can make sure that the software is safe, does not collect and does not transmit personal information.

  • Meduza OS installer

    The Meduza OS installer, you can get on your mobile devices without breaking a sweat. You can use any computer to run the installer. Simply plug in your device and follow the on-screen instructions. Then sit back and let your computer do all the work.

  • New approach

    Meduza OS offers a completely different approach to using your smartphone or tablet than other known operating systems. Meduza OS provides a reliable and stable base system used by millions of people on personal computers, servers, devices for the Internet of things.
